Fork Records

Fork Records was orginially the album series Fork, which the first album was Fork 1: The Original, which entered planning stages on November 7, 2018 when Ben's friend showed him an image of a plastic fork on the ground, and was released on April 14, 2019. The final album in the Fork series released on January 20, 2021. Fork Records has evolved into the group behind more music from Fork's creators.

Usage

Anyone is free to use this music for any personal use. It can be a school project, personal hobby, or even as a relaxing beat. It's free to use! However, anyone using the tracks for a commerical, money-making use is only allowed to do so with written permission. This includes content creators on YouTube and Twitch who make money from their respective platform. Written permission can be obained by emailing fork@benchase.info with your channel or organization details.
